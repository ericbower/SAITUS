Moving to the USA

The journey from SA to the US is a long and complex administrative process. Navigating the legal landscape is the first major challenge. Here are the essential official starting points and relocation guides:

About the Founders

Welcome to SAitUS.com! We are a Cape Town family who made the leap across the Atlantic in 1995. What started as a brave new adventure for my wife and me has turned into a 30-year journey across the length and breadth of the United States.

Having raised our three children here, we’ve experienced everything from the complexities of the immigration process to the simple, poignant joy of finding a box of Ouma Rusks in a random international market.

We’ve lived and traveled all over this vast country, but today, we are proud to call Austin, Texas home. We created this site to be the resource we wish we had back in '95—a digital "braai" where South Africans in the US can find a taste of home, a bit of guidance, and a reminder that no matter how far we roam, we’re never truly alone.

— The SAitUS Family

FROM CAPE TOWN TO AUSTIN, TX

🤠 Deep in the Heart of Texas

From the Mother City to the Live Music Capital of the World! Living in Austin since the mid-90s has taught us that while the landscape is different, the hospitality (and the love for a good fire) is exactly the same.

Austin "Braai" Culture

Texas BBQ and South African Braais are cousins. Whether you're smoking a brisket or grilling boerewors, the spirit of gathering remains the same. Check out the Austin Saffas meetups for local get-togethers!

The "Cedar Doctor"

We traded the Cape Doctor (SE Wind) for Central Texas Cedar Fever! A small price to pay for the incredible sunsets over Lake Travis that remind us so much of the Atlantic seaboard.

Where to Find Us

You'll often find us at Zilker Park or exploring the Hill Country. If you see a car with a Springbok decal in South Austin, give us a wave!
